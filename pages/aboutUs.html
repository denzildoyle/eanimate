About Us


e-Animate Solutions Limited is a technology solutions provider, operating in Trinidad & Tobago since 2009.

Our goal is to always be on par with the latest technologies, demonstrate how they can be used to simplify but yet enhance the end user's experiences, and offer it to our clients at the lowest possible cost.

The exposure, experience and expertise acquired by our team of professionals allows us to bridge the divide between theoretical academic constructs and the complex practical realities across Business and Technology, thus creating sustainable value for each client served.

Our Mission:

To deliver unmatched digital solutions through the employment of proven and cutting-edge technologies underpinned by superior customer service standards and integrated support services.
